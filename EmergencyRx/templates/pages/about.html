Our Mission

Closing the Information Gap
That Costs Lives

In Nigeria, the average time to find compatible blood during an emergency is 3–6 hours of manual phone calls — not because blood is scarce, but because nobody knows where it already is. EmergencyRx exists to close that information gap in seconds, not hours.

What We Do

We give hospitals, blood banks, and clinics a live inventory system, and give the public a single place to broadcast an urgent need to every verified facility nearby — instantly.

Who We Serve

Families in crisis, hospital staff managing perishable stock, and blood banks who want to be found by the people who need them most.

How We Measure Success

Every request that ends in "Found" instead of "Too Late" — tracked transparently on our public leaderboard and stats.
