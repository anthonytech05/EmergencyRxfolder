You submit a request specifying what you need (blood type, quantity, medication name) and your location. Our system instantly broadcasts that request to all registered hospitals and blood banks within your area. When a facility confirms availability, you receive their contact details and directions — in minutes, not hours.

Yes — creating a public account and submitting emergency broadcast requests are both completely free. Hospital registration starts at ₦10,000/month.

Our average response time is under 10 minutes during business hours. Facilities on our premium plan are required to respond to broadcasts within 5 minutes or their visibility ranking drops. Critical-urgency requests are highlighted and prioritized in the facility dashboard.

Facilities sign up by choosing the Hospital / Blood Bank Staff account type. Our team verifies the facility within 24–48 hours before it goes live. After verification, facility admins can update their blood and supply inventory in real time and earn visibility points for staying current.

The Emergency Card is a physical PVC card (same size as an ATM card) with your blood type, genotype, allergies, medical conditions, and emergency contacts. It has a unique QR code — when scanned by a first responder, it immediately shows your critical medical information even if you cannot communicate. Cards cost ₦1,000 each.
